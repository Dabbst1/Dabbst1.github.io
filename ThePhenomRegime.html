Questions every serious client should answer

These are the questions that separate casual interest from real commitment.

  • What is your main goal right now?
    Fat loss, muscle growth, strength, mobility, or consistency.
  • Why does that goal matter now?
    This shows urgency and emotional buy-in.
  • What have you already tried?
    It tells us what failed, what worked, and where you keep falling off.
  • What is really holding you back?
    Time, nutrition, inconsistency, injury, stress, or lack of structure.
  • How many days can you honestly train each week?
    Not the fantasy answer. The real answer.
  • Do you have injuries or physical limits?
    Programming has to be safe before it can be effective.
  • How solid is your nutrition?
    Most people sabotage results before training even gets a chance.
  • What kind of support do you want?
    Plans, accountability, form feedback, coaching calls, or full online coaching.
  • What timeline are you aiming for?
    Deadlines expose whether the goal is real or just talk.
  • Are you ready to invest in the process?
    Results cost time, effort, consistency, and money.
